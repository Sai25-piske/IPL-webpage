Team Bio

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is one of the most beloved teams in the IPL, known for its aggressive gameplay, loyal fan base, and legendary players like Virat Kohli and AB de Villiers. Despite not yet winning the IPL trophy, RCB remains one of the strongest and most iconic teams in T20 cricket.
