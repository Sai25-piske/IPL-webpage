Player Cricket Histories

Virat Kohli – Cricket History

Virat Kohli, one of the greatest batsmen in modern cricket, made his international debut in 2008. Known for his aggressive mindset, unmatched fitness, and consistency, Kohli has dominated all formats. In the IPL, he became the tournament's all-time leading run-scorer and served as RCB’s longest-running captain. His 2016 IPL season remains iconic with 973 runs.

Career Highlights:
• ICC Cricketer of the Decade
• 75+ International centuries
• Leading run-scorer in IPL history

AB de Villiers – Cricket History

AB de Villiers, also known as "Mr. 360°", debuted internationally in 2004 and quickly became one of cricket’s most innovative and explosive batsmen. With unmatched shot creativity, ABD has single-handedly won matches for RCB for over a decade. His partnership with Kohli is considered one of the best in T20 cricket.

Career Highlights:
• Fastest 50, 100 & 150 in ODIs
• Over 5000 IPL runs
• One of the most loved RCB players ever

Mohammad Siraj – Cricket History

Mohammad Siraj rose from humble beginnings to become a premier fast bowler for India. Debuting in 2017, Siraj is now known for his swing, seam movement, and aggression. For RCB, he has produced memorable spells including a spectacular Powerplay wicket burst in 2020.

Career Highlights:
• 2023 Asia Cup Final: 6-wicket spell
• Key bowler across formats for India
• One of the most improved pacers in IPL
